The

Fountain

The Fountain of the Dromedaries, is a splendid stepped baroque fountain characterized by the presence of two sculptures of dromedaries in a basket, made by Giovanni Battista Rainoldi.

DROMEDAR SCULPTURE


At one time the fountain was enriched by many other sculptures, which were destroyed when the complex was confiscated by the Austrians after the Borromeo family took part in the Cinque Giornate di Milan.




SCENOGRAPHY


The water falls from the stepped waterfall with a scenographic effect, and then passes to an elliptical basin once used as a fish pond.
